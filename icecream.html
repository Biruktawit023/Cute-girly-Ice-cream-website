Our Sweet Story

Ice Cream House started as a little dream in my kitchen. As a kid, I loved making ice cream with my grandma during summer vacations. Her secret recipes and loving approach inspired me to open my own shop.

Today, I still use her recipes but with my own creative twists! All our ice cream is handmade in small batches using locally sourced ingredients whenever possible.

My mission is simple: to create joy, one scoop at a time! Come visit us and experience the happiness that only homemade ice cream can bring.
